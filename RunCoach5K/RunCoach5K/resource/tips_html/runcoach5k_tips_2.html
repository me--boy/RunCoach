Function:

Improve landing technique, experience pawing movement, and enhance the flexibility of joints and movement frequency.

Practice methods:

  • Exchange the supporting leg with your toes not leaving the ground or holding the equipment with your two hands, weight should be supported from one leg to the other.
  • Speed up gradually from start place to Skips and Hops for 20 ~ 30 meters (yards).
  • Skips and Hops for 20 ~ 30 meters (yards).
  • Run from Skips and Hops to normal running for 40 ~ 60 meters (yards). Raise your thigh and enlarge steps gradually until run normally.
  • Run Skips and Hops to accelerative running for 40 ~ 60 meters (yards). Raise your thigh and enlarge steps gradually.


    • Notes:

    Upper body should be straight or slightly forward, raise center of gravity, pelvis ahead, keep entire body stretching. Press your thigh downside actively, relax your knee and ankle joints, put your calf forward naturally, land on your forefoot, finish the pawing movement, and then extend coxa, knee and ankle joint straight, at the same time swing your arms back and forth naturally.
