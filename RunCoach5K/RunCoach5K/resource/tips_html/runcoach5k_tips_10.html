Function:

Enhance speed and speed endurance.

Practice methods:

  • Repeat 5 ~ 7 times 30 meters (yards) with 90% strength, rest 30 seconds to 1 minute during interval.
  • Repeat 2 ~ 3 times 100 meters (yards) with 80% strength, rest 1 to 2 minutes during interval.
  • Repeat 1 ~ 2 times 150 meters (yards) with 70% strength, rest 2 to 3 minutes during interval.
