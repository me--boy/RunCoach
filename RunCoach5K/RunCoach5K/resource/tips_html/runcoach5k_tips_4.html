Function:

Experience push and swing technology. Enhance strength of coxa, knee and ankle joints when push on ground during running.

Practice methods:

  • Hops in place alternatively, experience the push movement, stretch joints of coxa, knee and ankle sufficiently, movement should be bouncy.
  • Leap forward above by small steps, experience push strength and cooperation of push and swing.
  • Back step for 20 ~ 30 meters (yards).
  • Run from back step to normal running naturally for 40 ~ 60 meters (yards).


    • Notes:

    Upper body should be straight or slightly forward, push and swing sufficiently and stretched.
