Function:

Enhance strength of leg muscles and the flexibility of joints and movement frequency.

Practice methods:

  • High leg lift in place for 30 seconds.
  • High leg lift steadily and not too fast for 20 ~ 30 meters (yards).
  • Run from High leg lift to normal running or speeding up running naturally for 40 ~ 60 meters (yards).


    • Notes:

    Upper body should be straight or slightly forward, raise center of gravity, pelvis ahead, keep entire body stretching. Raise your thigh alternately and high enough until thigh is straight with body, press your thigh downside actively, land on your forefoot, and then extend coxa, knee and ankle joints straight quickly, at the same time swing your arms back and forth naturally.
