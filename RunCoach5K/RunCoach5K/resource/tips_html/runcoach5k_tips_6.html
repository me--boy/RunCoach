Function:

Active from static state speedily and get maximum forward force.

Practice methods:

  • Run acceleratively after starting line, raise upper body gradually, and enlarge step stride and frequency. Arms swing firmly and actively.
  • Run from slow to accelerative naturally.
  • Run from hips and hops to accelerative running, forward upper body enough naturally.
  • Run from high leg to accelerative running naturally, speed step frequency and forward upper body gradually.

    • Notes:

    When accelerative running, enlarge step stride and frequency gradually. Arms swing firmly and actively.
