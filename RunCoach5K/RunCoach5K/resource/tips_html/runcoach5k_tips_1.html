If you can keep walking for more than 30 minutes, this tutorial will lead you to finish 5K running even you are a beginner.

Before starting, prepare a sportswear and a pair of comfortable shoes, you can also prepare a pair of sports headphone if you would like listening to music while running.

Warm up is necessary before start running, it will help to avoid injury to muscles or joints. It's normal that you will get some soreness after running, this symptom will gone after 3 or 4 days. But if there is swelling or sprains, you have to go to your doctor immediately.

Finally, we would like to provide some auxiliary training for your running; you can practise them in your free time.
