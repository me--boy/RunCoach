Function:

Experience the technology of leg folding and swaying forward after back step, enhance speed strength of ankle joint, improve body coordination ability.

Practice methods:

  • Leg kicking backward in place, experience leg folding movements.
  • Leg kicking backward running for 20 ~ 30 meters (yards). Let should kick backward sufficiently, speed should be not too fast.
  • Run from leg kicking backward to normal running naturally for 40 ~ 60 meters (yards).


    • Notes:

    Upper body should be straight or slightly forward, leg folding and swaying forward when leg kick backward sufficiently. Fold leg sufficiently, and keep swaying forward step small.
