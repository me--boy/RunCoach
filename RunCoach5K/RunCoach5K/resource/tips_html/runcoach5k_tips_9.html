Function:

Develop general endurance and running ability, learn and improve running technology, strength visceral organ function and develop speed feelings.

Practice methods:

  • Run in limited time without distance requirement. Run indoor in 5 minutes, 10 minutes, 15 minutes and then 20 minutes. Or 30 minutes outdoors running. Relax when running, no speed requirement, finish when time run out.
  • Finish regulated distance within limited time: finish 1500 meters (yards) within 5 minutes 20 seconds, then finish 5000 meters (yards) within 20 minutes.

    • Note:

    This method is mostly used to develop the sense of rhythm and speed.
